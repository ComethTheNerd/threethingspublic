Privacy Policy for Three Things!

Last updated: November 2025

Three Things! is an iMessage-only game that does not collect, store, share, sell, or transmit any personal data. All gameplay data is shared only between participants through Apple's iMessage system and never leaves your device or your iMessage conversation.

Data Collection

  • We collect no personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking
  • We do not use advertising or third-party SDKs
  • We do not store gameplay results or answers on any server

Children

This app can be used by anyone and contains no harmful content. However, gameplay answers are user-generated and may vary depending on participants.

Changes

If the app ever adds features involving personal data, this policy will be updated before any such changes are released.
